How Information Is Handled

Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. Hosting Stack UK aims to handle any information received through the website responsibly and transparently.

As a static informational website, Hosting Stack UK does not require visitors to create accounts, provide login details or submit payment information to access its public content.

The current Contact page displays a form interface, but no processing service or submission endpoint is connected. It therefore does not currently send the entered information to Hosting Stack UK. If a working contact method is introduced, information may be received when a visitor voluntarily uses it.

Information supplied through a future working contact method should be used for purposes connected with the enquiry, such as reading the message, preparing a response or maintaining necessary correspondence.

The website relies on hosting and technical services that may process limited technical information as part of delivering the site. The homepage also requests font files from Google Fonts, whose service operates under its own privacy terms.

Visitors who follow links to third-party websites should review the privacy policies and terms published by those providers.

Information Not Required

Information You Do Not Need to Provide

You can access the public website without supplying:

  • Usernames or account passwords
  • Payment-card information
  • Bank details
  • Government identification
  • Date of birth
  • Sensitive personal information
  • Customer account profiles
  • Purchase information processed directly by Hosting Stack UK

Please Take Care

Visitors should not send sensitive personal or financial information through ordinary email or general website enquiries. This information is not requested to view or use the public content.

Contact and Enquiries

Information You Choose to Provide

Visitors may choose to contact Hosting Stack UK using any working contact method added to the website. At present, the Contact page contains a form interface with no connected submission service, so it cannot transmit an enquiry.

If a working contact option is introduced, the information received may depend on what the visitor chooses to include and could involve:

  • A name
  • An email address
  • The content of the message
  • Any additional information the visitor chooses to include

That information should be used to read and respond to the enquiry, provide requested information, maintain necessary correspondence or protect the website from misuse where reasonably required.

Please avoid including passwords, payment details or other sensitive information in an ordinary enquiry. This policy must be reviewed when a contact-processing service is connected.
Accessing the Website

No User Accounts or Payments

Hosting Stack UK does not currently require visitors to create accounts, submit login credentials or provide payment information to access its public content.

The website does not directly process purchases, subscriptions or financial transactions.

Products and services discussed or recommended on the website may be purchased or accessed through third-party providers. Any account creation, payment processing or financial information involved in those transactions is handled by the relevant provider rather than Hosting Stack UK.

Each provider operates under its own:

  • Privacy policy
  • Terms and conditions
  • Security practices
  • Payment-processing arrangements
  • Cancellation and refund policies

Visitors should review those documents before completing a purchase or registration.

External Services

Third-Party Websites

Hosting Stack UK contains links to external websites, including hosting providers and other online services.

When a visitor follows an external link, they leave Hosting Stack UK and become subject to the destination website’s privacy policy, terms and technical practices.

Hosting Stack UK does not control how third-party providers collect, store, use or disclose information submitted directly to their websites. External providers may use account systems, payment processors, cookies, analytics, advertising technologies, fraud-prevention systems and customer-support platforms.

The inclusion of a link does not mean that Hosting Stack UK controls the provider’s privacy practices. Visitors should review the relevant third-party privacy information before creating an account, submitting payment details or providing personal information.

Website Improvement

Analytics and Website Usage

Hosting Stack UK does not currently use a visitor analytics platform within the website code inspected for this policy.

Analytics tools may be added later to help understand general website usage and improve content, performance and usability. If introduced, they may process information such as:

  • Pages visited
  • Approximate visit duration
  • General traffic source
  • Browser and device type
  • Screen size
  • Approximate location derived from technical information
  • Website interaction data

If analytics are introduced, this privacy policy should be updated to identify the relevant service and explain whether cookies or similar technologies are used.

Browser Technologies

Cookies and Similar Technologies

The static website does not currently appear to set non-essential analytics or advertising cookies through its own code.

The homepage requests externally hosted font files from Google Fonts. That service, as well as external websites reached through links, operates separately and may receive technical request information or use technologies under its own policies.

If analytics, embedded content or other cookie-based services are introduced later, this policy should be updated and any necessary consent mechanism should be implemented.

Use of Information

How Information May Be Used

If information is received through a future working enquiry method, supportable purposes may include:

  • Responding to enquiries
  • Maintaining necessary correspondence
  • Operating and protecting the website
  • Investigating technical problems
  • Improving website content and usability
  • Meeting applicable legal obligations where required

Hosting Stack UK does not seek to collect more personal information than is reasonably needed for the relevant purpose.

Information may be processed by service providers where necessary to operate email, hosting, security or other website functions. Those providers may process information under their own contractual and privacy arrangements.

Retention

Information received through enquiries should only be retained for as long as reasonably necessary to respond, maintain relevant records, resolve disputes or meet applicable obligations.

No fixed retention period or formal retention schedule is currently established in the project.

Protecting Information

Security

Reasonable steps may be taken to protect information received through the website. However, no method of online communication, hosting or electronic storage can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

Visitors should avoid sending passwords, payment information or other sensitive details through ordinary email or general contact methods.

The website is hosted and delivered using third-party infrastructure. Those providers may process limited technical data needed to deliver, secure and operate the service. The homepage’s externally hosted font request is also handled by a third-party service.

Your Information

Your Choices

Visitors may contact Hosting Stack UK to ask about personal information they have voluntarily supplied through a working enquiry method.

Depending on the circumstances and applicable law, a visitor may be able to request:

  • Information about how their details are being used
  • Correction of inaccurate information
  • Deletion of information that is no longer required
  • Restriction of certain uses
  • Withdrawal of consent where processing relies on consent

Requests may be subject to legal, security and record-keeping considerations.
